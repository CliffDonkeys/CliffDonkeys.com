Cliff Donkeys, a story of overtourism

Have you ever thought about the impact that tourism has on the environment? This is a website that helps to educate the general public about the impacts of overtourism. Welcome.

Cliff Donkeys has been created to build awareness to importance of responsible and sustainable tourism. Overtourism causes deteriorates fragile environments and disrupts the lives of local residents and businesses. Cliff Donkeys was created to help us all do our part to respect and replenish delicate destinations.
