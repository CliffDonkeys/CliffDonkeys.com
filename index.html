Cliff Donkeys, better manage tourism for all

Travel more aware of how tourism impacts small communities with limited resources and delicate environments. Cliff Donkeys exists to educate the general public about the impacts of overtourism.

Overtourism can cause deterioration of fragile environments and diminish the quality of life for locals; let's change that and do better by managing tourism.

Happy you're here, now let's get started.
